Analysis on Placement
Prediction Data set

Abstract

Getting a job after graduation is important for students. Many factors affect placement chances, such as academic performance, technical skills, internships, and communication skills. This project analyzes a Placement Prediction Dataset using Pandas Profiling to find key patterns. The goal is to understand how different factors influence a student’s chances of getting placed 

Introduction

Students prepare for placements by improving their technical skills, soft skills, and academic scores. Some students get placed easily, while others struggle. This project helps identify which factors play the biggest role in placements. By analyzing this dataset, we can predict placement chances and guide students on how to improve their employability.  

Literature Survey

Research shows that academic scores alone do not guarantee placements. Companies also look at internships, coding skills, projects, and communication abilities. Studies suggest that students with good soft skills and industry experience have better chances of getting placed. Data analysis and machine learning help in predicting placement outcomes based on historical data. Pandas Profiling provides quick insights
into important placement trends.

EDA & Project Overview

EDA helps in understanding placement trends. Pandas Profiling is used to analyze:

• Academic performance – CGPA and percentage impact.
• Technical skills – Certifications, coding skills, and projects.
• Internships – The role of industry experience.
• Communication skills – How soft skills impact placement.
• Correlations – Finding patterns between different factors and placement success
